\chapter{List of LHC Fills Used for Measurements}
\label{appendix:measurement_fills}

One can find in this Appendix the fully detailed list of \acrshort{LHC} fills used for the experimental campaign.
In each table, the fill number, measurement date, beam energy at the time of measurement, \gls{optics} configuration (\glssymbol{betastar} at IR\num{1} and IR\num{5}) and the purpose of the measurement are indicated.
